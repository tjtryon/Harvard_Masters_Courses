Midwest Motorsports FAQ

Q: Who is Midwest Motorsports?

A: Midwest Motorsports is a automobile tuning shop and racing team that specializes in tuning and racing German performance cars, specifically vintage VW and Porsche brands.

Q: Who is the people behind Midwest Motorsports?

A: TJ Tryon is the owner of both the tuning shop and the driver for the racing team.

Q: What are the specs on the race car?

A: The race car is a 1973 Porsche 914/4 with a 2.0L engine with dual webbers. The horsepower is approximatly 145 horsepower at the engine. The top speed is around 155 miles per hour.
